Concord Cat Program

Thanks to a generous anonymous donor, HAWS, Watertown Humane Society and the Humane Society of Jefferson County are able to offer FREE Outdoor Cat Clinics throughout the end of March 2023! This clinic is for residents of the Town of Concord, and will provide free spay/neuter surgeries, vaccinations, and deworming treatments to indoor/outdoor cats, barn cats, and feral cats residing in the town. Each location requires appointments for services, and hosts clinics on separate days throughout the week. Please review the information below and determine which shelter location will best suit your needs, and follow the link to book your appointment!
